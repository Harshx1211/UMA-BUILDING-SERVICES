# SiteTrack Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 2026

SiteTrack ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this Privacy Policy carefully.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Personal Data
We collect personally identifiable information that you voluntarily provide to us when registering for the App, including but not limited to:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Job title and role (e.g., Technician, Subcontractor)

### 1.2 Job and Field Data
As part of the primary functionality of the App, we collect data you enter regarding job sites, asset inspections, compliance defects, and client signatures. 

### 1.3 Offline Data and Device Permissions
To provide offline capabilities, we store data locally on your device using encrypted databases. The App will request permissions for:
- **Camera/Photo Library:** To capture visual evidence of compliance defects.
- **Location:** To map assets, provide route navigation to upcoming jobs, and confirm location signatures.
- **Local Storage:** To save generated PDF compliance reports and sync queues locally before syncing to the cloud.
- **Biometrics:** To secure access to the app locally via Fingerprint or Face Authentication. 

## 2. Use of Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain the App's tracking and compliance workflows.
- Manage user authentication and credentials securely via Supabase.
- Generate and export client-facing compliance reports and certificates.
- Facilitate background data synchronization and resolve data conflicts across devices for a seamless offline-first experience.
- Send you notification and job reminders.

## 3. Disclosure of Your Information

We will not share your personal information with any third party except:
- **As Required By Law:** Such as complying with a subpoena, or similar legal process.
- **Service Providers:** We may share data with third-party vendors who assist us in operating our infrastructure (such as Supabase for database hosting and authentication). 
- **Authorized Clients:** The primary mechanism of the app involves compiling job results and sharing them directly with authorized property management and clients.

## 4. Security of Your Information

We utilize enterprise-level security measures including local device data encryption and secure transmission protocols (TLS/SSL) when syncing with our backend to protect your personal information. However, no data transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage can be 100% secure.
